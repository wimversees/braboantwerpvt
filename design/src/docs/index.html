ETF 2017 Congress

The European Transport Workers’ Federation (ETF) organises its2013 Congress from 29 to 31 May 2013 in Berlin, Germany, on thetheme: “From Global Crisis to Global Justice – EuropeanTransport Workers Fighting Back”. Some 500 participantsrepresenting transport unions coming from 41 European countriesattend the ETF 4th Ordinary Congress.

Congress is the most important governing body of the ETF andelects the ETF President, the ETF General Secretary, the ExecutiveCommittee and the lay auditors.
